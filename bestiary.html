Bestiary

Baphet

A psionic bat-like creature. Roughly a foot long, the baphet is aggressive omnivore that live in caves and dense forests. Baphet have a sharp claw at the end of their wings and sharp teeth. Baphet use psionics to hunt prey in packs, baphet are able to communicate with each other up to 120 feet away via high pitch psionic waves. Baphets are omnivores, whose diet mainly consists of bugs and fruit.

Bhrulk

Bhrulk are large rock like insects. They have 6 legs and powerful stone colored chiton that guard their upper body. Bhrulk can quickly burrow into the ground to appear nearly indistinguishable from small boulders. Bhrulk tend to live in packs, but larger Bhrulk will move out and travel alone. Bhrulk are herbivores who will occasionally eat stone. These creatures have powerful stomach acids that can melt down the stone they eat into something digestable. Bhrulk can spew a blob acid towards their enemies. Bhrulk are very slow to start, but can reach high speeds if moving in a straight line.

Czardon

The czardon live in the Eridonian woods in nomadic tribes and are arggresive, attacking humans on sight. The czardon are rather intelligent and the most gifted of czardon can speak a rudimentary form of common, while all can signal thoughts though vocalizations and hand gestures. In the Age of Stories, a witch named Tabareth formed a pact with a tribe of czardon and taught them magic in exchange for their service. The czardon eventually killed Tabareth and consumed her and the teaching of simple magics would grow to become a custom in the czardon, usually equating to a few druids in large tribes. Czardon slightly alter depending on their spawning ground. Some hatch more lizard and salamander-like in deep woods. Czardon spawned by rivers, ponds, and lakes spawn into more fish, frog, alligator, or axolotl; in rare cases. Czardon spawn in 'litters' of nearly one hundred. Many of the czardon cannibalize each other while they are growing or when their tribemates die in combat.

Twaline

A Twaline is a two-headed mountain lion like monster that casts spells. Each heads has a small chitinous horn and twaline have slits on their neck that look like gills that release a paralytic mist into the air. Twaline are known to be territorial, solitary, and avoidant of civilization. Great at being stealthy, the Twaline is an apex predator. The horns of the Twaline attract lightning. When the Twaline is damaged by lightning, the horns absorb the damage.

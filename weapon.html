Weapon System: Modifcations and Alterations

Rules

The Weapon System allows for weapon smiths to make modifications and alterations to your weapons to enhance their capabilities. Each weapon can have one modification, and one alteration. Modifications offer a wide variety of effects but some of them are restricted to certain weapon damage types, or archetypes. When a restriction is listed as "All", this modification may be applied to any simple or martial weapons (shields and bows/crossbows not included). Alterations can be applied by any Journeyman, Master, or Artisan smiths. Alterations allow for Breaks, which trigger when using the weapon and rolling over the targets armor class by the listed threshold. This activates an additional effect and the threshold can be reduced by meeting the alterations listed trigger. Keywords and conditions are explained in the Keywords and Conditions section of this page.
